Transportation

From the Airport

The best way to travel from the Austin Airport to your hotel or the Commons Conference Center (CCC) is by Lyft, Uber, or Taxi (25-40 minutes depending on traffic). Note that there are bus routes but they take 1.5-2.5 hours for this route.


From the Domain to CCC

The Domain is conveniently located close enough to the CCC to walk. Walking times are:
> 13 minutes from Lone Star Court
> 16 minutes from Element Austin
> 28 minutes from Archer Hotel


To/From Downtown

The JJ Pickle Campus is conveniently located near 3 bus lines and is a short walk to the rail station.
Please check out Cap Metro for full details, schedules, lines, and fare information.

For purchasing information, visit https://www.capmetro.org/howtobuy

  • UT Faculty, Students, Staff can use their ID and ride for free
  • Visitors can:
    • > Use cash, but note that exact change is required
    • > Download the Umo App and it to pay
    • > Purchase a reloadable fare card at HEB (grocery store)


Bus Lines

Links to maps and schedules are provided below.
Be sure to select Northbound or Southbound as it pertains to your trip.
> Route 803 - 45 minutes to downtown. Pick up on Exploration Way right outside Commons Conference Center.
> Route 466 - local line that circles the Domain area. Pick up on Exploration Way right outside Commons Conference Center.
> Route 3 - 55 Minutes to downtown. Pick up on Burnet Rd.
> Route 383 Pick up on Burnet Rd.


Rail Line

Austin has one lightrail line that runs from downtown to McKalla Station, which is located on the east side of Q2 Stadium and is a 20-minute walk from the Commons Conference Center.
> Rail 550 - 30 minutes to downtown.


Parking

There is free parking for participants at the conference venue. You will need to place a parking pass on your dash - either one you received via email or there will be some copies available at registration. The pass contains a map of the parking locations.
